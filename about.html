About the Training & Placement Department

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology & Sciences (ANITS) has a dedicated Training & Placement Department led by experienced faculty coordinators. The department focuses on preparing students for successful careers through technical training, aptitude development, communication skills, group discussions, mock interviews and campus recruitment drives.

The placement department collaborates with leading multinational companies, organizing workshops, seminars, internships and industrial visits. Students receive continuous guidance throughout their academic journey, ensuring they are industry-ready.

Our Department Focuses On

  • Career Guidance & Counseling
  • Aptitude Training
  • Technical Skill Development
  • Communication & Soft Skills
  • Mock Interviews
  • Resume Building
  • Industrial Visits
  • Campus Recruitment Drives
